About Us

Care at Every Step

Meet Satkar Group: Your Trusted Partner

As a company, what distinguishes us from the competition is the robustness and breadth of our in-house expertise.

At Satkar Group, we don't just build structures; we craft dreams, design lifestyles, and create spaces where memories are made. With a legacy spanning over 25 years, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the real estate industry. Our journey has been marked by a relentless commitment to excellence and an unwavering focus on our clients' aspirations.

Our Story

Founded in 1995, Satkar Group emerged from a vision to transform urban living. Over the years, we have evolved and adapted to the changing needs and preferences of our clients. Today, we stand tall as a symbol of quality, innovation, and reliability.

Our Mission

Delivery Quality: We take pride in our commitment to delivering homes and projects of the highest quality. Each development is a testament to our dedication to craftsmanship and attention to detail.
Innovate Continuously: We embrace innovation and stay at the forefront of industry trends. From design to technology, we constantly seek new ways to enhance the living experience.
Build Communities: Beyond bricks and mortar, we create communities. Our projects are designed to foster a sense of belonging, where neighbors become friends and families thrive.
Sustainably Develop: We recognize our responsibility to the environment. Our sustainable practices and eco-friendly designs reflect our commitment to a greener future.

At Satkar Group, we are more than just real estate developers. we are creators of spaces where dreams come to life.
